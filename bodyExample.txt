<p>How would you like to start out the new year with old money? A new study finds Orange County is one of the top counties in the nation with unclaimed funds.</p>
    ]
    <p>Orange County, which finishes No. 9 on the list with $519,982,055 in unclaimed property, is sandwiched between No. 8 Kings County, New York, and No. 10 San Diego, according to <a href=&quot;http://www.SmartAsset.com&quot; target=&quot;_blank&quot;><u>SmartAsset.com</u></a>.</p>
<p>&quot;That&#39;s enough to pay one month&#39;s rent on 26,357 Orange County apartments,&quot; says SmartAsset&#39;s Asees Singh of the County of Orange&#39;s total.</p>
<p>Topping the SmartAsset list is Los Angeles County with $3,887,525,183 in unclaimed funds.</p>
